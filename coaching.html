Coaching

My offering for coaching services is directed at people who want to change their situation at work or in their everyday lives. I offer long-term coaching processes or short-term, topic-oriented appointments, depending on the need. People discover the path toward personal change by understanding personal desires and analyzing everyday situations. I will provide methods to help implement these changes in the situation at work or in everyday life.

I offer:

  • Business coaching
  • Personal coaching
  • Coaching for management
  • Change consultation
  • Diversity coaching
  • Gender coaching

Aims of coaching

  • Empowering people
  • Improving the work–life balance for people
  • Discovering solutions for interpersonal conflicts or difficult situations
  • Improving ways of handling discrimination
  • Offering career prospects
  • Helping people in situations where they are overwhelmed
